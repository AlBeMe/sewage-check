Sewage Check

Check Thames Water sewage outflows near the Kennet & Avon Canal, River Kennet and River Pang.

Install this app
Android: Chrome menu → Install app  |  iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen

Search with a postcode
Postcode: e.g. SN8 2BG or SN82BG (space optional)

Search with lat/lng
Coordinates: e.g. 51.423770,-1.717683

How to get lat/lng from Google Maps
Long press a spot, swipe up the panel, copy the numbers

Cookies remember your last search
Already filled in next time — just tap Check

Status colours
Red = discharging | Amber = recent 48h | Green = clear | Grey = offline
